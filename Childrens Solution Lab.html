What is Children's Solution Lab about?

The International Day for the Eradication of poverty (IDEP) is a day for awareness, activism and advocacy, actions against poverty, marked every year on 17th October. The theme of 2020 was "Acting Together To Achieve Social and Environmental Justice For All"

By working towards social and environmental justice, communities have the opportunity to address the underlying root causes of child poverty, at a structural level. It will also enable communities to tackle the impacts of the COVID-19 pandemic on children.

We worked with a small groups of children to identify issues and propose solutions to those issues.

Donation Drive

After the success of the programme. The group organized a donation drive for the people living in Chiwong, Mongar.

Some of the activities of the project are:

  • Distribution of food and shelter items to he children and families
  • Awareness and sensitization programmes on child poverty and its impact
  • Participation in GNRC and other international programmes( such as IDEP, DPAC, children's day etc)
  • Any other engagement programmes that comes up with us or our partner organizations.
