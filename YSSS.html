Youth Society for Social Service



Youth Society for Social Service (YSSS) is an informal community based organization aimed at engaging youth and adolescents in community services and supporting community development as a whole.


YSSS shall strive to reach out rural communities around the country and embrace making a model community by 2030. Our partners and members include any group of people seeking to make a difference. Currently, a large number of unemployed youth looking for employment have participated in the events, hoping to learn by doing and willing to contribute to the community through social service.

Objectives

The objective of YSSS shall be but not limited to:
  • To promote education, advocacy and literacy of the community
  • To create a forum for engagement of children, youth, elderly and women for social and communal harmony
  • To contribute to sustainable green economy and smart agriculture in the community
